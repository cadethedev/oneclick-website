Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

oneclick is a Chrome extension that uses AI to answer homework questions in Google Docs. This policy explains what data is used and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

oneclick does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not operate user accounts, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

Your API Key

To use oneclick, you provide your own Gemini API key from Google AI Studio. This key is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage and is never sent to our servers. It is only used to make requests directly to Google's Gemini API on your behalf.

Document Content

When you click the oneclick button, the text content of your open Google Doc is sent to our backend (hosted on Supabase) solely to generate an answer. This content is:

  • Transmitted over HTTPS
  • Used only to fulfill your immediate request
  • Not stored, logged, or retained after the response is returned

Sentence Rewrites

If the optional Sentence Rewrites feature is enabled, individual sentences from the generated answer are sent to our backend to be rephrased. The same rules apply — sentences are not stored or logged.

Local Storage

The following settings are saved locally on your device and never leave it:

  • Your Gemini API key
  • Typing speed and accuracy preferences
  • Rewrite settings and custom prompt
  • In-progress session state (cleared after use)

Third-Party Services

oneclick interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API — processes your document text to generate answers. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — hosts the backend functions that relay requests. No user data is persisted.
  • Google Docs — the extension reads and types into documents you have open. No data is sent to Google beyond normal Docs usage.

Children's Privacy

oneclick does not knowingly collect any information from anyone. No personal data is collected from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
